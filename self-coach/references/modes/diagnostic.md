# Mode: diagnostic

Runs a self-diagnostic for the leader across the critical dimensions of the transition to the next leadership level.

## When to use

- Periodic check (recommended: monthly)
- Before updating the career plan
- When feeling stuck or scattered
- After a significant milestone (promotion, major delivery, executive feedback)

## Flow

### 1. Read full context

- `profile.md`
- `operating-model.md`
- `coach-state.md` → current standing + Open Commitments + Mode Usage
- `coach-log.md` → full diagnostic history (this mode legitimately needs the time series)

### 2. The Five Leadership Dimensions

For each dimension, assess based on EVIDENCE (not intention):

| Dimension | Diagnostic question | Scale |
|---|---|---|
| **Scope** | Am I operating at the portfolio level or still at the IC level? | 1-5 |
| **Strategy** | Do I have a documented and communicated Product Vision? | 1-5 |
| **People** | Are my direct reports growing in a measurable way? | 1-5 |
| **Business fluency** | Can I articulate the P&L of my products? | 1-5 |
| **Stakeholders** | Do I have documented influence at the executive level? | 1-5 |

Scale:
- 1: Haven't started / no evidence
- 2: First steps / anecdotal evidence
- 3: Consistent progress / partial evidence
- 4: Strong / clear and recurring evidence
- 5: Ready for Head / unequivocal evidence

### 3. Framework: Ravi Mehta — Head Competencies

Read `references/frameworks/ravi-mehta-competency.md`.

Focus on the 4 competencies that gain the most weight in the transition:
- **Business Outcome Ownership** — accountability for business results, not features
- **Strategic Impact** — influence on the company's direction, not just the product's
- **Team Leadership** — multiplying people, not IC
- **Managing Up** — managing the relationship with CEO/C-level

For each one: "What is your strongest evidence today? And what is your clearest gap?"

### 4. Cross-reference with Profile

Check `profile.md`:
- Which mapped tensions are currently active?
- Which blind spots are showing up in the diagnostic?
- Cross-reference with the profile: is an accumulate-and-over-process pattern causing procrastination on any dimension?

### 5. Profile Trap Check

Based on the scores, flag which profile traps are most likely active:

| Score Pattern | Likely Trap | Guardrail to Reinforce |
|---|---|---|
| High Strategy, Low Stakeholders | Reasoning through invisible leaps, not externalizing — conclusions stay in their head | G3: Write takeaway in 1 sentence |
| High People, Low Business | Comfort zone — developing others instead of developing self | G4: Force business-fluency or exec-sim |
| High Scope, Low Strategy | Doing IC under the label of "portfolio management" | G1: Commitment to delegate 1 IC task |
| All dimensions 2-3 (no 4+) | Broad but shallow — a novelty-chasing tendency, too many fronts | G7: Pick 1 dimension to push to 4 this quarter |

### 6. Pre-Mortem

Ask: "It's [target date]. The promotion didn't happen. Looking at today's diagnostic — which dimension most likely held you back?"

### 8. Save

Append the full diagnostic to `coach-log.md` (the historical archive).
Then refresh `## Current Standing` in `coach-state.md` with the new scores, active tensions, top priorities and provocation — this is the snapshot every other mode reads.
If either file doesn't exist: create with header + first entry.

Offer: "Do you want to update the career plan (`career-plan.md`) based on this diagnostic?"

Before closing, run the SKILL.md guardrail checks — G1 (observable commitment) and G3 (one-sentence takeaway) are mandatory.
